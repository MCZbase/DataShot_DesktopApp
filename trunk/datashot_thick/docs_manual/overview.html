Description

This software implements suggestions from a 2006 workshop on efficient capture of data in Natural History Collections. It is designed for capturing data from specimen labels in an insect collection. It seeks efficiency through separation of specimen handling (imaging of the specimens and labels) from capture of the data on the labels into a database (off of the images rather than the labels), and through automation of the capture of some of the data (barcode numbers for specimens, and current identifications from printed unit tray labels).
